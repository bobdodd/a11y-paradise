About A11y Paradise

My name is Bob Dodd. I have spent over 25 years working in digital accessibility — as a web developer, as head of accessibility at the Canadian National Institute for the Blind (CNIB), and as an academic researcher in adaptive user interfaces.

This project exists to make sure that practical, hard-won accessibility knowledge doesn't retire when I do. Everything here is freely available:

  • Training materials covering standards, development techniques, testing, and organizational practices
  • Open-source tools for accessibility testing and auditing
  • Curated resources from the wider accessibility community

The source for all of this lives at a11y-paradise on GitHub.
